Welcome reception is 6-8 pm on Sunday in JCIT, 13th floor.

  • ‍Restaurant Chinese Garden on the 4th floor of Communal Building right next to JCIT has seats for about 200 participants
  • ‍Other participants get their meals in bento boxes from Chinese Garden

Conference dinner is on Tuesday 7:30-9:30 pm at Lamma Rainbow restaurant at Lamma Island, about 20 minutes off from Hong Kong Island It is located in the village of Sok Kwu Wan, Lamma Island, New Territories, Hong Kong. http://lammarainbow.com Bus transport to a ferry taking us to Lamma departs from Ground Floor (GF) of JCIT 6 - 6:30 pm. For more detail about the restaurant, see http://dis2018.org/venues.html.
